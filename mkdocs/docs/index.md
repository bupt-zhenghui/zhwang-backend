## About Me

I'm a third-year graduate student at AI School, Beijing University of Posts & Telecommunications. I received bachelor degree from International School, BUPT. My research interests include software engineering and computer vision. Currently I am studying multi-modal image harmonization under the supervision of Fangxiang Feng.

During my undergraduate years, I worked as an intern in the algorithm department of Nice, during which I was mainly responsible for the design of fake sneakers recognition algorithm. After that, I joined the Data platform department of Bytedance, where I was responsible for the development of the data governance portal. During the postgraduate period, I worked as an intern in Meituan Home Business Group, mainly responsible for the development of the merchant side event presentation system.
